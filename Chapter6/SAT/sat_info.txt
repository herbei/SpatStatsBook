
US Average SAT scores by state in 1999
------------------------------------------------------------

oname:  An abbreviated state name.

vscore: The average verbal SAT score, by state for 1999.

mscore: The average math SAT score, by state for 1999.

pc:     The percentage taking the exam.

name:   The name of the state



Ref:

M. M. Wall (2004) A close look at the spatial structure implied by the
CAR and SAR models. Journal of Statistical Planning and Inference,
121:311–324.
